Executive Summary

Property-management SaaS products optimize operations. Marketplace apps optimize discovery and lead volume. Mimarek can become more attractive by owning the full property transaction journey for Saudi developers, brokers, and real estate operators.

Positioning

Make the marketplace a trusted transaction layer for verified inventory, not a separate browsing feature.

Product Shape

Turn every inquiry into managed work: lead, qualification, viewing, offer, reservation, contract, settlement, and post-sale operations.

Go-To-Market

Lead with seller trust, developer launch pages, buyer confidence, and operational conversion after the sale.

Market Signals

The strongest competitors show five repeatable patterns. These patterns are the source material for the recommendations below.

Unified operations are the SaaS baseline

AppFolio, Buildium, DoorLoop, Yardi Breeze, Zillow Rental Manager, and Apartments.com bundle leasing, rent collection, maintenance, screening, owner or resident portals, listings, and online workflows. Mimarek should present marketplace as part of the same operating system.

Discovery is becoming data-led

Property Finder surfaces transactions, area insights, mortgage tools, new projects, agent search, and buyer guides. Bayut Saudi highlights true photos, real prices, real properties, map search, commute search, 360 tours, new projects, and Tru Broker discovery.

Trust signals drive conversion

Saudi context makes this even stronger. REGA supports FAL license lookup, real-estate advertisement license lookup, broker contracts, and real estate inquiry services. Trust can be a product feature, not just a legal check.

Government journeys shape buyer expectations

Sakani presents targeted services for broker, developer, buyer, renter, self-build, and project discovery. Ejar anchors rental trust around electronic contracts, payment follow-up, licensed brokers, and handover documentation.

Mimarek Foundation

The current marketplace already has serious rails. The ideas in this report extend these rails with better discovery, packaging, intelligence, and conversion.

Existing capability Current evidence in repo Strategic implication
Listing lifecycle MarketplaceListingStatus: draft, pending review, published, under contract, sold transferred, expired, rejected, suspended. Mimarek can support professional publishing and moderation without rebuilding listing state.
Buyer-safe listing view apps/web/lib/marketplace/listing-view.ts returns allow-listed view models and excludes seller-internal fields. Future public or semi-public marketplace surfaces can grow from a safe cross-org read layer.
Inquiry workflow MarketplaceInquiry has open, converted, withdrawn, declined, closed won, and closed lost states. The lead-to-deal pipeline is already modeled and can become a seller CRM cockpit.
REGA and FAL attestation OrgRegaAuthorization tracks license number, seller or buyer role, verification status, method, expiry, and verifier. Trust badges, seller profiles, and gated actions can use the existing authorization concept.
Settlement controls Unit transfers require deed proof, manual verification, signed sale contract, both-org REGA verification, and a platform conveyance switch. Mimarek has a rare high-trust transaction rail that pure listing portals do not usually offer.
PII protection Marketplace customer and deed proof paths use encryption and audited PII reads. Buyer trust and compliance can be marketed as a platform strength, provided UX stays transparent.
Admin moderation /dashboard/admin/marketplace moderates listings, REGA authorization, deed proofs, and conveyance settings. The marketplace can scale with review queues and risk scoring rather than ad hoc staff review.

Idea Portfolio

Each idea is written as a product brief. Value and effort are directional, using High, Medium, or Low.

01

Verified Saudi Listing Badge

Trust Compliance Seller quality
HighValue
Benefit

Increases buyer confidence, reduces spam or low-quality listings, and gives sellers a reason to complete compliance data. It turns regulatory readiness into a visible conversion signal.

Scope
  • Verified ad license display.
  • Verified seller authorization display.
  • Compliance status on cards and detail pages.
  • Moderator checklist with expiry tracking.
Persona

Buyer organization admin, acquisitions manager, broker, marketplace moderator, and seller manager who wants higher inquiry quality.

Risks

False confidence if verification is stale, legal exposure if the badge overstates the check, and staff workload if every listing needs manual review.

Priority

Start now. This is the clearest Saudi-specific differentiator and fits existing moderation rails.

How to implement in Mimarek

Extend MarketplaceListing with verification metadata such as adLicenseVerifiedAt, adLicenseExpiresAt, verificationSource, and verificationNotes. Keep manual attestation first using the existing admin moderation page, then make VerificationMethod.API_VERIFIED useful later if REGA or FAL integration becomes available. Add badges to marketplace cards, listing detail, seller my-listings, and admin review tables. Add a scheduled stale-check job that moves expired verification into pending review.

02

Seller Trust Profile

Reputation Broker enablement Developer brand
HighValue
Benefit

Makes the marketplace feel safer and more professional. Sellers gain a profile they can improve, while buyers can compare credible suppliers beyond price.

Scope
  • Seller profile page.
  • REGA or FAL status.
  • Response time and inquiry closure rate.
  • Active listings, projects, languages, locations.
Persona

Broker owner, developer sales director, buyer acquisition team, and Mimarek platform staff.

Risks

Ranking disputes, incentive gaming, privacy issues around operational metrics, and reputational liability if ratings are not clearly defined.

Priority

Near term, after verified listing badges. Start with objective metrics before subjective ratings.

How to implement in Mimarek

Create a MarketplaceSellerProfile model or derive the first version from Organization, OrgRegaAuthorization, listings, and inquiries. Add computed fields through a read model: median response time, open inquiry count, converted inquiries, closed won deals, and verification status. Add profile links from listing cards and filters. Avoid star ratings at first; use objective trust facts and a clear "Verified by Mimarek" language.

03

Listing Quality and Compliance Score

Supply quality Seller guidance Moderation speed
HighValue
Benefit

Improves listing quality before publication, reduces moderator back-and-forth, and gives sellers clear actions that increase visibility and conversion.

Scope
  • Completion checklist.
  • Photo and document requirements.
  • Pricing and duplicate risk warnings.
  • Publish readiness score.
Persona

Seller agent, sales manager, marketplace moderator, and operations manager responsible for clean inventory.

Risks

Overly strict scoring could block valid listings. AI-generated feedback could be wrong. Score must not become a hidden ranking black box.

Priority

Quick win. It uses fields Mimarek already has and can be delivered before advanced AI.

How to implement in Mimarek

Add a pure scoring helper in apps/web/lib/marketplace/listing-quality.ts. Score required data, title length, price, area, city, district, short address, ad license, description, photos when media exists, and duplicate listing risk. Surface the score in PublishListingDialog, my-listings, and admin moderation. Store optional snapshots only if performance requires it; otherwise compute from listing data.

Keep project publication behind the same moderation and REGA review posture.

Marketplace Inquiry to CRM Pipeline

Conversion CRM Sales operations
Very HighValue
Benefit

Pure marketplaces stop at the lead. Mimarek can turn each inquiry into a managed sales process with ownership, next action, reminders, stages, and reporting.

Scope
  • Automatic CRM lead creation.
  • Lead source, listing, budget, city, and buyer org.
  • Pipeline stages for inquiry, viewing, offer, reservation, contract, won, lost.
  • Task reminders and SLA.
Persona

Sales manager, agent, broker, developer CRM admin, and executive tracking marketplace ROI.

Risks

Duplicate customers, PII mishandling, noisy pipeline records, and confusion between buyer organization and individual contact.

Priority

Immediate. This is the heart of making marketplace native to Mimarek.

How to implement in Mimarek

Enhance confirmMarketplaceInterest and convertMarketplaceInquiryToDeal so seller-side CRM records include source metadata, listing ID, buyer organization, contact name, normalized phone, assigned owner, and next action. Keep using the canonical encrypted customer path. Add a Marketplace source filter to CRM views and a dashboard widget for incoming inquiries that need action today.

Very HighValue
Benefit

Risks

Reservation and Offer Workflow

Transaction Reservation Deal control
Very HighValue
Benefit

Turns buyer interest into controlled commitment. Reduces leakage to informal channels and gives sellers a structured way to accept, reject, counter, or reserve.

Scope
  • Submit offer.
  • Counter offer.
  • Reserve unit.
  • Attach documents and proof of seriousness.
Persona

Buyer acquisition manager, seller sales manager, broker, finance/admin user, and legal reviewer.

Risks

Legal enforceability of reservation, payment handling, cancellation disputes, and race conditions if two buyers act on one unit.

Priority

High, but gate carefully. Mimarek already has reservation and transfer models that make this plausible.

How to implement in Mimarek

Add MarketplaceOffer linked to inquiry and listing with amount, conditions, status, expiry, and audit trail. Use transactions and the existing state machine to prevent double-reservation. Let accepted offers call the existing conversion path into reservation. If booking fees are added later, do not store payment as a generic note; model it through finance/payments with refund and audit states.

Risks

Recommended Roadmap

Sequence the work so trust and conversion improve before heavier marketplace intelligence. This avoids building advanced matching on top of weak listing quality.

Phase 1

0-45 days

Verified badges, listing quality score, seller profile v1, marketplace-to-CRM metadata, and WhatsApp deep-link templates.

Phase 2

45-120 days

Project pages, richer media, seller performance cockpit, saved searches, and stronger inquiry stage management.

Phase 3

120-240 days

Price-per-sqm comps, affordability calculator, reservation and offer workflow, and moderated project launch campaigns.

Phase 4

240+ days

External data integrations, API verification where available, financing partners, syndication, and marketplace liquidity optimization.

Risk Controls

The marketplace touches compliance, cross-tenant data, financial decisions, and irreversible transfer workflows. These controls should be treated as product requirements.

Compliance Overclaim

Badge copy must say exactly what was verified, when, by whom, and whether it was manual or API verified. Do not imply government endorsement unless an official integration supports that claim.

Cross-Tenant Leakage

Keep buyer-visible marketplace views behind explicit allow-listed view models. Any new analytics or seller recommendations need aggregation thresholds.

PII and Consent

Continue using encrypted customer creation paths. Add consent capture for WhatsApp or marketing follow-up and audit every sensitive read.

Pricing Liability

Mark estimates as guidance, show data confidence, and keep external data licensing clean. Avoid presenting price intelligence as formal valuation.

Offer and Reservation Disputes

Model offer expiry, cancellation, refund status, and approval history explicitly. Do not rely on notes for legally meaningful states.

Operational Load

Use risk scoring and quality checklists to reduce moderator workload before adding more supply. Staff queues need SLA, assignment, and batch review.

Measurement Framework

Mimarek should measure whether the marketplace is becoming more attractive through trust, liquidity, conversion, and operating efficiency.

Supply Quality

Percent of listings verified, average quality score, rejection reasons, median moderation time, and percentage with rich media.

Demand Quality

Inquiries per listing, duplicate inquiry rate, qualified inquiry rate, saved searches, and buyer repeat usage.

Conversion

Inquiry to viewing, viewing to offer, offer to reservation, reservation to contract, and contract to settled transfer.

Seller Performance

Median first response time, SLA breach rate, closed-won rate, expired listings, and price-change impact on inquiry rate.

Platform Safety

Suspensions, compliance failures, stale verifications, PII read audits, and reported listing issues.

Business Value

Marketplace-sourced deals, seller retention, paid upgrade adoption, project launch revenue, and downstream module activation after deal close.
